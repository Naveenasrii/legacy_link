Connecting Generations of PSG Tech

Our Mission

Legacy Link is built to foster meaningful connections between alumni and current students of PSG College of Technology. We aim to promote mentorship, collaboration, and knowledge-sharing across generations.

About Legacy Link

Legacy Link is an alumni-student interaction platform developed as a community-driven initiative. It supports communication between PSG Tech students and alumni, while also enabling senior-junior interaction for academic, career, and social growth.

About PSG College of Technology

PSG College of Technology, founded in 1951 in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, is a premier engineering institution affiliated with Anna University. Known for excellence in education, research, and innovation, PSG Tech boasts a large and accomplished alumni network across the globe.
