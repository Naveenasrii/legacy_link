PSG COLLEGE OF TECHNOLOGY

Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu

About the College

PSG College of Technology, founded in 1951, is one of the most prestigious engineering institutions in India. Managed by the PSG & Sons' Charities Trust, it is affiliated with Anna University and known for its excellence in education, innovation, and research. PSG Tech offers a wide range of undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering and applied sciences.

Achievements

  • Top Rankings: Consistently ranks among the top 50 engineering colleges in India by NIRF.
  • Academic Autonomy: Granted academic autonomy by Anna University, enabling flexible and industry-relevant curriculum.
  • Research & Innovation: Houses over 30 research centers with numerous funded projects and patents.
  • PSG-STEP: Science and Technology Entrepreneurial Park that supports startups and innovation.
  • Industry Collaborations: MoUs with global companies like Siemens, Bosch, TCS, Infosys for training and projects.
  • Accreditation: NAAC 'A+' grade and NBA-accredited programs.
  • Global Outreach: Academic exchange programs with leading international universities.
  • Placement Success: Excellent placement records with top recruiters like Google, Amazon, and Microsoft.

Notable Alumni

  • Dr. Mylswamy Annadurai – Former ISRO Scientist
  • Gopal Srinivasan – Chairman, TVS Capital
  • Numerous Leaders and innovators in major tech and business sectors globally
