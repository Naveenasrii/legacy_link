Shiv Nadar Padma Bhushan

Founder & Chairman of HCL Technologies

B.E. Electrical and Electronics Engineering - PSG College of Technology (PSG Tech), Coimbatore (1967)

Honors: Padma Bhushan (2008), Forbes Billionaires List

Current: Chairman Emeritus, HCL Technologies | Founder, Shiv Nadar Foundation

Shiv Nadar is the visionary architect of India's IT revolution and one of the country's most celebrated entrepreneurs. As the founder of HCL in 1976, he pioneered the computer hardware industry in India before transforming the company into a global IT services powerhouse. With a personal mission to "create value through innovation," Nadar built HCL into a $12 billion enterprise with operations in 50+ countries. His philanthropic initiatives through the Shiv Nadar Foundation have established premier educational institutions impacting thousands of students annually.
"The biggest risk is not taking any risk. In a world that's changing quickly, the only strategy that is guaranteed to fail is not taking risks."
