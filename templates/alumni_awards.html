PSG College of Technology

Alumni Awards & Scholarships

Overview

PSG College of Technology recognizes and honors its outstanding students and alumni through various awards and scholarships. These accolades are instituted by alumni, faculty, and well-wishers to encourage academic excellence and commemorate notable contributions.

For a comprehensive list, visit the official Alumni Scholarships page.

Notable Alumni Awards

Proficiency Award
Awarded to the student who secured the first rank in each course.
Best Outgoing Student Award
Presented to the best outgoing final year student in each programme.
Koorathalwar Award
Instituted in 1956-57 by the 1957 batch in memory of Mr. S. Koorathalwar for the best outgoing B.E./B.Tech student.
Precitex Award
Established in 1979 for meritorious students in the subject of 'Spinning'.
Sri Balaguruva Chettiar Memorial Award
Started in 1980 for the best outgoing Civil Engineering student.
Nachiarammal Educational Trust Award
Initiated in 1983 for the top Electrical and Electronics Engineering student.
U.S. Krishnarajan Memorial Endowment Award
Begun in 1983 for the best Metallurgical Engineering student.
Krishnammal Narayanaswamy Memorial Award
Founded in 1983 for the best MBA student.
GRD Memorial Award
Instituted in 1983 for conducting the Endowment Lecture.
S.A.S. Krishnan Chettiar Memorial Award
Established in 1984 for the best B.Sc. Computer Technology student.
B.Tech. Alumni Award
Started in 1985 by Thiru D. Lakshminarayanasamy for the best outgoing B.Tech student.
Kalluri Proficiency Award
Initiated in 1985 for the top Electrical and Electronics Engineering student.
Sugavanam Venkatesan Memorial Endowment Award
Begun in 1985 for the best Electronics and Communication Engineering student.
Jayaprabakaran Memorial Award
Established in 1985 for the best MBA (full-time) student.
GTV Endowment Deposit
Instituted in 1986 by Mr. G.T. Venkataswamy Naidu.
Smt. Palaniammal Award
Started in 1987 for the best VII Semester Mechanical/Production Engineering student.

Additional Information

These awards not only recognize academic and professional excellence but also serve as a testament to the enduring legacy of PSG Tech's alumni. They inspire current students to strive for excellence and uphold the values of the institution.
